Q-TIE

Quantum Theory, Information & Entanglement

Q-TIE is a local research collective focused on quantum entanglement and its applications in quantum information and communication. Our mission is to foster collaboration, discussion, and learning in one of the most exciting areas of quantum theory and computing.

We explore topics including:

  • Quantum information and computing
  • Entanglement theory
  • Quantum cryptography
  • Quantum Communication
  • Quantum machine learning

We welcome students, researchers, and faculty with interests in any related area. If you'd like to join or collaborate, please contact us.
